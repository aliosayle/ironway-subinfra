Responsible AI

Scope of AI Use

Celtic Verbs AI implements AI strictly as an integration and operational optimization layer. Our systems are scoped to data retrieval, workflow coordination, and pattern detection within industrial parameters.

Human Oversight

We operate on a "Human-in-the-Loop" architecture. AI models are constrained from autonomous decision-making in critical paths. Every automated action is accompanied by a human threshold.

Auditability

Decision paths are traceable. We maintain immutable logs for model inputs and retrieval context. Our governance framework ensures that every AI-supported event is auditable.
